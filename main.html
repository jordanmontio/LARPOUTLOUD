Y2K FINANCE

3D (FREELANCE)

In my role at Y2K Finance, I was charged with creating an engaging 3D promotional video to elucidate the specialized offerings of a unique suite of structured products designed for exotic peg derivatives. Y2K Finance provides market participants with robust tools for hedging or speculating on the risk associated with specific pegged assets deviating from their ‘fair implied market value’.

The challenge here was to visually represent the abstract concepts of exotic peg derivatives and the risk hedging process. I was entrusted with the responsibility of transforming these complex financial mechanisms into a captivating and understandable narrative that could be easily grasped by both seasoned traders and new market participants.

Working in close collaboration with a team of financial experts, I was able to gain a deep understanding of the products and the unique solutions they offered. This, coupled with my skills in 3D animation, allowed us to develop a compelling visual story that effectively communicated the value proposition of Y2K Finance.

This experience not only refined my abilities in 3D animation and visual storytelling, but also deepened my understanding of complex financial products. By successfully translating these intricate concepts into a captivating promotional video, I was able to contribute to Y2K Finance’s mission of making sophisticated financial products accessible to a wider audience.
